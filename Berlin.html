BERLIN.

Berlin as a skate city – it’s first of all a myriad of spots and skaters! The city is so big that a fresh flow of new spots and skate crews that no one had ever heard about keeps coming up. Yet, the vibe is really friendly regardless of where you go or who you meet.

Reasons that makeBerlin so attractive are aplenty: culture left and right; every happening artist or musician has Berlin far up on their hip-list – plus the city’s quite unique history, in which the divide and later reunion just form the latest of many moving chapters.

Famous skate spots include - Heidelberger Platz, Skatehalle Nike SB Shelter, Kulturforum and Skatepark Vogelfreiheit, to name a few!
