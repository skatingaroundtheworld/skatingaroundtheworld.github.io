PARIS.

Writers, poets, and artists have been inspired by the City of Lights for centuries. Skateboarding there is no different. While it has never had the hype of Barcelona, Paris has held its own in the Skate scene for decades.

The abundant marble of Le Dôme, Bercy, or La Défense, and perhaps even the legendary Eiffel Tower "Basins" crossed with the rich Bohéme lifestyle of cafés, Le Métro, and multiethnic cuisine keep Paris up on any skateboarders “must visit” list.

Famous skate spots include - Creteil, La Défense, Bercy and Le Dôme, to name a few!
