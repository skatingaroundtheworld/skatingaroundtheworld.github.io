LONDON.

When it comes to terrain and aesthetic, London has something that the skateboarding world has always found captivating. Although the city has never been gifted in the same way as it’s continental counterparts with open spaces and smooth ground, it does however, have many unique spots surrounded by a panoramic halo of interesting visuals.

What makes London so great for skating is the Tube. London’s underground network means you can buy a one-day travelcard and traverse the city all day long.

Famous skate spots include - Southbank, St.Pauls, Bay Sixty 6 and Euston Banks, to name a few!
