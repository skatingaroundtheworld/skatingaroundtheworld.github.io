BARCELONA.

The capital of skateboarding in Europe, the Mediterranean Mecca, call it what you want, Barcelona is a skateboarding paradise.

Barcelona experienced a golden era of skating back in the 90s when it seemed like every skater flocked here for cheap living and absolute freedom to skate anywhere and today, both amateurs and professionals travel from all four corners of the globe to experience skating in Barcelona. It is important to point out that in 2006 a law was introduced allowing the local police to prosecute skateboarders, so be mindful!
